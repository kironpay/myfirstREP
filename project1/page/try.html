Story

Solo Leveling is a South Korean novel written by Chugong, which tells the story of Sung Jin-Woo, a weak hunter who becomes the strongest hunter in the world.

Characters

  • Sung Jin-Woo: The main protagonist, a weak hunter who becomes the strongest hunter in the world.
  • Cha Hae-In: A skilled hunter and Jin-Woo's friend.
  • Lee Ju-Hwan: A powerful hunter and Jin-Woo's rival.

Adaptations

Solo Leveling has been adapted into various forms of media, including:

  • Manhwa: A South Korean webtoon illustrated by Dubu.
  • Anime: A Japanese anime series produced by A-1 Pictures.
  • Web Novel: The original novel written by Chugong.
